Launch of Non-Formal Education (NFE) Documentary “The Light of Education - Leaving No One Behind!”

Details
Published: 12 November 2019

In commemoration of His Majesty the Fourth Druk Gyalpo’s 64th birthday, the Non-Formal and Continuing Education Division(NFCED), with the financial support from Korean National Commission for UNESCO launched a video documentary “The Light of Education - Leaving No One Behind!” at the Department of Youth and Sports(DYS) on 11th November 2019.

The objectives of producing the video documentary are:

- To pay tribute to His Majesty the Fourth King, the pioneer of NFE in Bhutan.
- To advocate and inform mass public about NFE programme through media.
- To celebrate the achievements of NFE.

The documentary was also broadcasted in the National Television (BBS2) intended to promote higher visibility and publicity besides celebrating success stories of NFE.

Felicitation programme for Medal winners and certificates of excellence

Details
Published: 02 October 2019

The Department of Adult and Higher Education, Ministry of Education organized a felicitation programme on 2/10/2019 to honour and recognize the excellence achieved by six undergraduate scholarship students who returned with gold medal and certificates of excellence. His Excellency Sherig Lyonpo felicitated the students with Tashi Khadar and congratulated thescholars for their stellar performances and achievements. The programme was also graced by Hon’ble Secretary, Ministry ofEducation and officials from the Department of Adult and Higher Education.
